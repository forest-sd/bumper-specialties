Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our bumper repair services.

How long does a typical bumper repair take?

Most minor repairs (scratches, scuffs, small dents) can be completed in 2-4 hours. More extensive damage like cracks or collision repair typically takes 1-2 business days including paint curing time.

Is it better to repair or replace a damaged bumper?

In most cases, repair is both more cost-effective and faster than replacement. We always evaluate repair first and only recommend replacement when the damage is too severe for a reliable repair.

Will the paint match my car's color?

Yes. We use computerized spectrophotometry to match your vehicle's exact current color, accounting for age and environmental fading. The match is virtually undetectable.

Do you work with insurance companies?

Absolutely. We work with all major insurance providers and can assist with documentation, estimates, and the claims process to make your repair as smooth as possible.

How much does bumper repair cost?

Costs vary depending on the type and extent of damage. Minor scratch repair may start around $150, while more significant repairs can range from $300-$800. We provide free estimates for all work.

Do you offer fleet or dealer pricing?

Yes, we have dedicated programs for dealerships, rental agencies, and fleet operators with volume pricing, priority scheduling, and dedicated account management.

Can you fix a cracked plastic bumper?

In many cases, yes. We use specialized plastic welding and reinforcement techniques to restore structural integrity to cracked bumpers before refinishing them.
