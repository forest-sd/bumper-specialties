How to Care for Your Plastic Bumper

Modern plastic bumpers are designed to absorb impact and protect your vehicle, but they still need proper care to maintain their appearance and functionality.

Regular Cleaning

Wash your bumper regularly with automotive soap and water. Avoid harsh chemicals that can degrade the plastic or strip protective coatings. A microfiber cloth prevents scratching.

Wax and Protect

Apply a quality automotive wax or sealant to your bumper every few months. This creates a protective barrier against UV rays, road debris, and environmental contaminants.

Address Damage Early

Small scratches and chips may seem minor, but they can worsen over time. Moisture can penetrate cracks and cause the plastic to deteriorate. Early repair prevents bigger problems down the road.

Parking Smart

The majority of bumper damage occurs in parking lots. Leave extra space, use pull-through spots when possible, and be mindful of shopping cart corrals and tight spaces.
