The Science Behind Perfect Paint Matching

One of the biggest concerns with bumper repair is whether the paint will match. Modern paint matching technology has made this concern largely a thing of the past.

Computerized Spectrophotometry

We use spectrophotometers that analyze your vehicle's exact paint color by measuring how light reflects off the surface. This captures not just the base color, but metallic flake size, pearl effects, and color-shift properties.

Multi-Stage Systems

Modern automotive paints often use multiple layers—primer, base coat, mid-coat, and clear coat. Our matching process accounts for each layer to ensure the final result is indistinguishable from the original.

Environmental Factors

Paint fades and changes over time due to sun exposure, washing, and environmental factors. Our technicians account for this aging process, matching your vehicle's current color—not the factory spec from years ago.

The Result

When performed correctly, a professional color match is virtually undetectable. That's the standard we hold ourselves to at Bumper Specialties.
